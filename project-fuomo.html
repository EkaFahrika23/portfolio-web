FUOMO Creator Monetization Platform

FUOMO is a web-based platform that enables digital creators to share content and monetize their work through fan support, paid content, and membership features. The system allows creators to manage their content, track supporter transactions, and monitor earnings through a centralized dashboard.

Role: Quality Assurance (QA Engineer)
Platform: Web-Based Application
Industry: Creator Economy / Digital Content Monetization

Key Features Tested

  • User registration, login, and creator profile management
  • Content upload and content access control
  • Supporter purchase and payment transactions
  • Paid content access validation for supporters
  • Creator dashboard and earnings tracking

Testing Approach

  • Requirement-based testing to ensure alignment with product requirements
  • End-to-end testing for creator-to-supporter workflows
  • Regression testing after new feature releases
  • Exploratory testing to identify usability issues and unexpected behaviors

Outcome & Impact

  • Ensured stable and reliable platform functionality before production release
  • Improved reliability of creator content publishing and supporter transaction workflows
  • Reduced system errors through structured testing and validation
  • Supported successful deployment of the platform for creators and supporters
