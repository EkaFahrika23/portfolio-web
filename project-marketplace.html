Marketplace Integrated Accounting System

The Marketplace Integrated Accounting System is a web-based accounting application designed to manage financial transactions from online marketplaces such as Shopee and Lazada. The system automatically synchronizes sales data, records financial transactions, and generates financial reports to support business accounting processes.

Role: Quality Assurance
Platform: Web-Based Application
Industry: Financial Management

Key Features Tested

  • Marketplace product and stock data synchronization (Shopee & Lazada)
  • Automatic revenue recording from marketplace transactions
  • Journal entry automation and transaction reconciliation
  • Financial reporting and sales summary dashboards

Testing Approach

  • Integration testing for marketplace API (Shopee & Lazada)
  • End-to-end testing for sales-to-accounting workflows
  • Regression testing for omni-channel stock synchronization
  • Data validation for automated financial reporting

Outcome & Impact

  • Ensured data accuracy between marketplaces and accounting records
  • Identified and resolved critical multi-platform sync edge cases
  • Improved reliability of automated stock update processes
