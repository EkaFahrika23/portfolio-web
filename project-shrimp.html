Shrimp Farm Accounting Management System

Shrimp Farm Accounting Management System is a web-based accounting application developed specifically for shrimp farming companies to manage financial transactions, operational costs per pond, revenue tracking, and financial reporting. The system provides accurate, transparent, and structured financial control across shrimp farm operations and company management.

Project Type: Website
Team Size: 1 Project Manager, 1 Backend Developer, 1 Frontend Developer, 1 QA
Role System: Superadmin, Admin, Staff income, Staff expense

Scope & Responsibilities

  • Defined project scope, timeline, milestones, and deliverables
  • Gathered accounting and operational requirements from stakeholder
  • Coordinated backend, frontend, and QA teams
  • Managed sprint planning, task allocation, and progress tracking
  • Ensured accounting logic aligned with shrimp farming business processes
  • Managed risks, change requests, and issue resolution
  • Coordinated UAT, deployment, and system handover

Key Features Delivered

  • Financial transaction recording (income & expenses)
  • Operational cost tracking per shrimp pond and cultivation cycle
  • Chart of Accounts (CoA) and accounting journal management
  • Automated financial reports (profit & loss, cash flow)
  • Accounting dashboard for management monitoring

Outcome & Impact

  • Established a structured and reliable accounting system for shrimp farm operations
  • Simplified monthly accounting and reconciliation processes
  • Improved visibility of operational costs and farm profitability
  • Enabled faster, data-driven financial decision-making for management
  • Project delivered on schedule and successfully adopted by finance and farm teams
