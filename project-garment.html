Garment Manufacturing Management

Sales, Purchase & Inventory Management System is a web-based application designed to manage sales transactions, purchase orders, supplier data, and stock inventory. The system provides real-time stock visibility, transaction tracking, and reporting to support accurate operational and financial control.

Project Type: Website
Team Size: 1 Project Manager, 1 Backend Developer, 1 Frontend Developer, 1 QA
Role System: Superadmin, admin

Scope & Responsibilities

  • Defined project scope, timeline, milestones, and deliverables
  • Gathered requirements from stakeholder
  • Coordinated backend, frontend, and QA teams
  • Managed task allocation, sprint planning, and progress tracking
  • Acted as liaison between stakeholders and development team
  • Handled change requests, risk management, and issue resolution
  • Coordinated UAT, deployment, and system handover

Key Features Delivered

  • Sales transaction and invoice management
  • Purchase order and supplier management
  • Stock-in and stock-out tracking
  • Real-time inventory balance and stock alerts
  • Sales, purchase, and inventory reporting dashboard

Outcome & Impact

  • Centralized sales and stock data into a single reliable system
  • Reduced stock discrepancies through structured transaction control
  • Accelerated daily transaction recording and reporting
  • Improved management decision-making with real-time dashboards
  • Project delivered on time and fully adopted by business users
