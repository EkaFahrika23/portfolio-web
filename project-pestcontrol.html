Pest Control Management System

Pest Control Management System is a web-based application designed to manage pest control service operations, including customer management, service scheduling, technician assignments, and service reporting. The system helps operational teams track service activities, monitor job progress, and maintain accurate service records.

Role: Quality Assurance (QA Engineer)
Platform: Web-Based Application
Industry: Field Service / Pest Control Operations

Key Features Tested

  • Customer data management
  • Service scheduling and technician assignment
  • Service execution tracking and status updates
  • Service reporting and activity logs

Testing Approach

  • Requirement-based testing to validate business workflows
  • End-to-end testing of service scheduling and execution processes
  • Regression testing after system enhancements
  • Exploratory testing to identify edge cases and usability issues

Outcome & Impact

  • Ensured system reliability for managing pest control service operations
  • Improved accuracy of service scheduling and technician assignment workflows
  • Reduced operational errors through validated system processes
  • Supported smooth system implementation for operational teams
