Online Product Sales System

Online Product Sales System is a mobile and web-based application designed to manage online product sales, including product catalog management, customer transactions, order processing, and payment handling. The system allows customers to browse products, place orders, and complete payments through the mobile application, while administrators manage products, orders, and reports through the web dashboard.

Role: Quality Assurance
Platform: Mobile Application & Web-Based System
Industry: E-Commerce / Online Retail

Key Features Tested

  • Product catalog browsing and search functionality
  • Shopping cart management
  • Checkout and payment processing
  • Order management and order status tracking
  • Admin product and order management dashboard

Testing Approach

  • Requirement-based testing to validate business workflows
  • End-to-end testing of product purchase processes
  • Cross-platform testing between mobile and web systems
  • Regression testing after system enhancements
  • Exploratory testing to identify usability issues

Outcome & Impact

  • Ensured stable functionality of mobile and web e-commerce platforms before production release
  • Improved reliability of product purchase and order processing workflows
  • Reduced transaction errors through structured testing and validation
  • Supported smooth system deployment for customers and business operations
